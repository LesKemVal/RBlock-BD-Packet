    R. Block Nation – Reg CF Launch Checklist¶

    Powered by Kore + Regulated Funding Portal

    This checklist outlines the key steps and documents required to complete the Reg CF capital raise for R. Block Nation. The offering will be hosted on a white-labeled funding portal and embedded into our own platform UI and domain. This raise is the first live campaign on the R. Block Nation platform and serves as the foundation for a JOBS Act-compliant digital securities experience.

    📄 Foundational Company Documents¶

    • [ ] Articles of Organization (PDF)
    • [ ] EIN Letter (IRS confirmation)
    • [ ] Cap Table (CSV or Excel)
    • [ ] Business Address and Contact Info
    • [ ] Company Pitch Deck or Overview Summary

    🔐 Control Persons & Ownership Info¶

    • [ ] Government-issued ID for Founder/Principal
    • [ ] Control person contact details
    • [ ] Ownership percentage breakdown (Class A / Class B)
    • [ ] Disclosures of any prior securities offerings (if applicable)

    📑 Legal Disclosures & Reg CF Requirements¶

    • [ ] Form C (Draft – to be filed by Funding Portal)
    • [ ] Use of Proceeds Statement
    • [ ] Risk Disclosures
    • [ ] Revenue Model and Financial Overview

    Note: These will be reviewed and finalized within the funding portal prior to SEC Form C filing.

    🧰 Platform Integration & Setup¶

    • [ ] Register issuer profile on Kore
    • [ ] Upload supporting documentation into the Kore dashboard
    • [ ] Connect Kore account with white-labeled funding portal
    • [ ] Embed investment workflow into R. Block Nation domain/UI
    • [ ] Assign roles: Admin, Legal, CFO (as applicable)

    🔁 Final Compliance Review¶

    • [ ] Confirm all required documents are submitted via Kore
    • [ ] Escrow account initiated by funding portal
    • [ ] Review and approve offering page on embedded portal UI
    • [ ] Form C officially filed with SEC by the portal

    ✅ Once all steps are complete, R. Block Nation is ready to publicly launch the Reg CF raise through the embedded investor portal, with fully compliant onboarding, token issuance, and post-investment tracking.

    This process is Reg CF compliant and powered by Kore + a FINRA-regulated portal (e.g., North Capital).
